Joypad · Wireless firmware update

Reboots the controller into BLE OTA DFU — no USB needed.

Idle. Make sure the controller is on and advertising.
  1. Click the button, pick your Joypad SInput device.
  2. It reboots and advertises as AdafruitDFU.
  3. In nRF Connect → connect to AdafruitDFU → DFU → push joypad_ota.zip (make ota-zip).

Recovery if an update won't boot: double-tap reset → UF2 drive → drag a .uf2.
